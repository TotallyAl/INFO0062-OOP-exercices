What are the classes defined in this Java program ?
- Item, Groceries, GroceriesList

What are the instance variables of these classes:
- Item: label, quantity, unitPrice
- Groceries: items
- GroceriesList: no instance variables

What are the operations perfomed by the main method ?
- The main method instanciates the class Groceries and different Items(Mineral Water, Apples, Egg)
- For each item instanciated by the class Item, there is: a label of the item; a price and a quantity
- The main method inserts the items: mineral water and apples in the groceries object
- The main method modifies the quantity of eggs from 6 to 12.
- The main method inserts the Item egg in the groceries object
- The main method prints the price and the contains of the Groceries object, that contains different quantities of different items.
